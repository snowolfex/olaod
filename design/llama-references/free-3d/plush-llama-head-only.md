# Plush Llama Head-Only Brief

## Goal

Create a plushy llama mascot that reads clearly as a head or bust first, with enough softness and charm to feel premium and friendly instead of flat or generic.

## Core look

- oversized plush head
- fluffy forehead tuft
- upright rounded ears
- short rounded muzzle
- bead-like glossy eyes
- very soft silhouette

## Framing

- crop just below the neck
- optional tiny scarf, bow, or stitched collar line
- keep the head centered and symmetrical enough for app branding use
- allow slight tilt for personality, but not so much that it hurts logo readability

## Material direction

- velvety plush or felted fabric
- subtle seam detail
- very light fabric nap texture
- soft studio lighting
- no shiny plastic toy finish

## Best palette

### Preferred
- wool: `#F3E8D6`
- face: `#D8C0A3`
- nose: `#6B4E3D`
- eyes: `#2A1E18`
- accent: `#5E8E88`

### Alternate
- wool: `#F6F2EA`
- face: `#C9A47D`
- nose: `#7A5942`
- eyes: `#241A16`
- accent: `#7EA9B8`

## Expression

- sweet
- calm
- a little curious
- approachable for first-time users

## Avoid

- pink body or hot accent colors
- realistic fur strands
- long narrow muzzle
- angry eyebrows
- over-detailed wrinkles
- direct Ollama brand mimicry

## Best use cases

- app splash art
- onboarding moments
- help/manual illustrations
- launcher icon studies
- empty-state mascot
