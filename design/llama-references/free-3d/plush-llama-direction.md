# Plush Llama Direction

## Chosen base vibe

- Soft plush toy llama
- Rounded, friendly silhouette
- Big simple eyes
- Short muzzle
- Chunky wool volume
- Head-first mascot readability

## Important constraint

- Do not use pink as the main body color

## Recommended palette directions

### Option A: Cream + oat
- body wool: warm cream
- face and ears: soft oat
- nose: cocoa brown
- eyes: glossy espresso
- accents: muted sage or dusty teal

### Option B: Cloud white + caramel
- body wool: off-white
- face and ears: light caramel
- nose: walnut brown
- eyes: dark brown
- accents: desaturated sky blue

### Option C: Light mocha + cream
- body wool: pale mocha
- face and ears: cream
- nose: deep taupe
- eyes: near-black
- accents: muted moss

## Shape notes

- oversized plush head relative to body
- small stubby legs
- slightly oversized upright ears
- rounded forehead puff
- gentle plush seam lines if needed
- soft toy proportions over realism

## Expression notes

- curious, sweet, approachable
- tiny smile or neutral plush mouth
- wide friendly eyes
- no aggressive or angular features

## Head-only adaptation

If this becomes a head or bust instead of a full-body mascot:

- keep the fluffy forehead tuft
- keep both ears visible and upright
- exaggerate the muzzle slightly
- crop just below the neck bow/scarf area if an accessory is added
- preserve plush softness in the silhouette

## Avoid

- bright pink body color
- realistic fur rendering
- harsh specular plastic shading
- overly thin legs or neck
- direct copying of Ollama brand geometry or face proportions

## Good fit for Oload

This direction should feel like:

- plushy
- local-first
- friendly
- slightly premium
- mascot-like without looking childish in a cheap way
