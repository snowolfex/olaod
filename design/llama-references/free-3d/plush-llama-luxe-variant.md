# Plush Llama Luxe Variant

## Intent

Keep the plush softness, but push the character slightly upward in polish so it feels more like a boutique premium mascot and less like a generic toy-store plush.

## Changes from the base plush direction

- slimmer silhouette without losing softness
- richer cream and camel value separation
- less exaggerated plush seams
- cleaner facial planes
- slightly calmer, more composed expression
- more premium accent materials and colors

## Still keep

- oversized head
- fluffy forehead tuft
- upright ears
- rounded muzzle
- dark glossy eyes
- plush fabric feel

## Luxe palette

- wool: `#EFE2D0`
- face: `#CDA989`
- nose: `#654739`
- eyes: `#201714`
- accent 1: `#486B72`
- accent 2: `#8C6F4F`

## Material direction

- matte velour plush
- subtle felt transitions
- almost no visible coarse fibers
- cleaner seam placement
- more studio-product-photography discipline

## Expression

- gentle
- knowing
- calm
- quietly charming

## Avoid

- clownish proportions
- candy colors
- oversized kawaii blush marks
- noisy accessories
- overly childish face spacing
