# Plush Llama Prompt Variants

## Prompt 1: Premium mascot render

`cute plush llama mascot, soft stuffed-animal styling, warm cream wool, oat face and ears, cocoa nose, glossy dark eyes, fluffy forehead tuft, rounded muzzle, upright ears, premium toy proportions, subtle seam lines, soft studio lighting, cozy premium product render, not pink, not realistic fur, not plastic`

## Prompt 2: Head-only brand study

`plush llama head mascot, centered bust portrait, oversized fluffy head, soft cream wool, caramel face panels, tiny embroidered smile, dark espresso eyes, premium felt texture, muted teal accent scarf, clean 3d brand study, high readability silhouette, not pink, not realistic animal fur, not glossy plastic`

## Prompt 3: Friendly app companion

`adorable plush llama companion character, soft rounded toy form, cream and oat palette, short plush legs, fluffy top tuft, gentle curious expression, cozy matte fabric texture, subtle stitching, simple premium 3d illustration, mascot for local AI workspace, non-pink palette, no realism, no hard edges`

## Negative prompt ideas

- `hot pink`
- `realistic fur strands`
- `plastic toy shine`
- `harsh shadows`
- `angry expression`
- `thin neck`
- `overly detailed anatomy`
- `logo copy`
