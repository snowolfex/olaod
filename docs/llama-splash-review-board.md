# OL Icon Review Board

This review board replaces the earlier mascot concept work with the shipped OL mark direction.

## What is approved

- A ringed O with the L integrated into the right side.
- Warm copper and cream finishes that align with the generated app and installer icons.
- Theme variation through lighting and accent handling, not through different silhouettes.

## What to check

- The O still reads instantly at small sizes.
- The L does not look detached from the ring.
- The mark feels stable on light, tech, and dark backgrounds.
- Help and PDF illustrations use the same geometry as the app icon.

## Rejected directions

- Any animal or character graphic.
- Any mascot face, ears, muzzle, or character accessories.
- Any variant that adds enough decoration to make the mark read like clip art instead of product branding.
