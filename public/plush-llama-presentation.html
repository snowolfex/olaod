Oload Mascot Direction

Plush Llama,
Not Pink

The chosen mascot lane is a plushy llama with premium stuffed-animal softness, warm cream and oat neutrals, cocoa facial contrast, and a muted dusty-teal accent. It should feel calm, local-first, friendly, and readable at both hero and small supporting sizes.

Chosen vibe: plush mascot Primary palette: Premium Cream Constraint: no pink body tones Best fit: Help, PDF, splash, empty states

Visual Rules

Non-Negotiables

1
Oversized head

Keep the plush head dominant so the mascot reads instantly.

2
Forehead tuft

This is the easiest recognition cue to keep stable across scenes.

3
Non-pink neutrals

Stay in warm cream, oat, cocoa, espresso, and muted teal.

4
Plush, not plastic

Soft velour/felt texture beats toy-store gloss every time.

Oload Usage Map

Where This Should Show Up

Help Surface

Use one calm bust or half-body plush llama at the top of the guide and keep section art secondary to the copy.

PDF Manual

Use simplified bust mascots per major section, posed toward text or cards, with low scene complexity for readability.

Walkthrough

Use the plush llama only for intro, checkpoint, and final reassurance moments. Let the UI stay primary during spotlight steps.

Splash / Hero

This is the fullest scene. Use the richest lighting and the most personality here, but keep the overall atmosphere calm and premium.
