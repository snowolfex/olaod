Presentation board

OL icon,
not the old mascot.

This board replaces the previous mascot presentation. The shipped direction is now a product mark: warm, compact, recognisable, and consistent across launch surfaces, docs, PDF exports, the taskbar icon, and installer flows. 

premium OL monogram for a local-first AI workspace, rounded O ring with the L integrated into the right side, warm cream and copper finish, soft editorial gloss, compact silhouette, high small-size legibility, clean product-brand geometry, light tech and dark variants through finish only, no mascot features, no animals, no plush styling, no copied third-party brand art

Use it

  • Auth and welcome surfaces.
  • Help headers and PDF export illustrations.
  • App icons, installer icons, and launch affordances.

Avoid it

  • No animal or plush-character artwork.
  • No split-letter layouts that break the monogram.
  • No excessive detail that hurts favicon readability.

Implementation note

The immediate product requirement is consistency: the same OL geometry should appear in Help, exported guides, and public-facing brand boards.
