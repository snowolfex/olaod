Reference board

OL brand reference board.

This board replaces the earlier mascot reference lane. The current brand system is the OL monogram: one strong O ring, one integrated L, one silhouette that stays stable across app chrome, docs, installers, and exported guides.

Keep

Integrated geometry

The L must feel locked into the right side of the O instead of floating next to it.

Warm material finish

The copper-cream balance should match the generated app and installer icons.

Theme stability

Light, tech, and dark variants should change finish, not silhouette.

Reject

  • Any animal, plush, or mascot artwork.
  • Any version where the O and L separate into two weak shapes.
  • Any overly detailed render that collapses at favicon or PDF scale.
