Extracted Traits

What to borrow and what to avoid

This is the useful part. If the next concept pass follows these constraints, it will land much closer to Ollama instead of generic llama clip art.

Keep the silhouette blunt and readable. Big wool mass, simple neck and head shapes, minimal tiny accessory clutter, and forms that still read at favicon or splash-card size.
Favor flat illustration over faux-3D mascot rendering. Official Ollama visuals read as clean editorial cartoon work, not gaming mascots, plastic stickers, or plush-toy realism.
Eyes matter more than costume. The official material is friendly because the faces are simple and readable. A good llama here needs stronger eye and mouth decisions before theme decoration.
Theme variants should come from attitude and palette first. Plain, tech, and dark should mostly be pose, accent color, and small props. Do not solve them with over-designed accessories.
Avoid generic vector-stock problems. No hyper-cute eyelashes, no rainbow party clutter, no oversaturated sticker gradients, and no ultra-thin limbs that look like clip-art leftovers.
